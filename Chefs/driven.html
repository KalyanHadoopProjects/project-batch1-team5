ABOUT KOSUS

Kosus Hospitality Alliance has been established to enhance the careers of hospitality professionals, through international exposure with the leading brand opportunities. Understanding the needs of F&B services and production personnel, we at Kosus Hospitality Alliance with a very efficient and skilled team in both consulting and hospitality sections, strive to magnify opportunities through global cultural exposure.

Being an establishment by Food and Beverage professionals, Kosus Hospitality Alliance helps the participants in opting for the best of the available paths in the industry. We have our helping hand for enthusiasts, who wish to go for professional, trainee and intern programmes and as well for front and back office and housekeeping in the Indian domicile and regions abroad.

We trust the inter link between the participants and the industry is the respect for the profession. We provide complete guidance about the chosen work place, either in the cities of India or foreign nations.

Kosus Hospitality Alliance believes, creative skills result in diversified yet composed outcomes. It is which inspires us to fetch the talents and introduce them to the global food cultures.
